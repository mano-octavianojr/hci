Coursework

  • Course Project (100%)

Application Design for Visually Impaired Users

Students will form teams of 2-3 members and develop a concept for a smartphone app that provides audio-based navigation assistance for visually impaired users. The app will offer features such as real-time location tracking, obstacle detection, and voice-guided directions to help users navigate their surroundings safely and independently.

Objectives

  • Market Analysis: Research existing navigation apps and technologies for visually impaired users, identifying gaps and opportunities for improvement.
  • User Interface (UI) Design: Create wireframes and mockups of the app's interface, ensuring it is accessible and user-friendly for visually impaired users.
  • Implementation Plan: Provide a step-by-step plan for implementing the app, including estimated timelines and resource requirements.
  • Project Presentation: Prepare a presentation summarizing the target user and the objectives.

Schedule of presentation and grading criteria will be announced in class.

Deliverables

  • Milestone #1: A 1-2 page summary of the market analysis.
  • Milestone #2: Initial draft of the UI design.
  • Final Presentation: A detailed presentation covering the following:
    • Target user
    • Market analysis results
    • Complete user interface design
    • Proposed implementation plan
